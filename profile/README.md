## 🔷 Overview

**Plex Earth for AutoCAD** is a professional mapping and terrain-enhancement extension built for civil engineers, surveyors, architects, GIS specialists, infrastructure planners, and CAD professionals who require real-world geographic data directly inside AutoCAD workflows.

The addon is designed around satellite imagery integration, terrain extraction, contour generation, coordinate-based modeling, elevation analysis, topographic drafting, site planning, and GIS-assisted engineering precision.

It is commonly used for road planning, land development, environmental studies, infrastructure mapping, route optimization, terrain visualization, construction preparation, and engineering-grade geospatial drafting.

For Windows users operating inside AutoCAD-driven design environments, Plex Earth for AutoCAD simplifies map-based workflows while improving terrain accuracy and geographic productivity.
